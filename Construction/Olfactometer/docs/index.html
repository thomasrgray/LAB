Overview #

Click to view CAD model

This is the documentation for building and using a olfactometer. This project was developed in the Wachowiak Lab at University of Utah’s implementation and detailed documentation was done in the Katz Lab at Brandeis University.

Features #

Useful instrument for odor delivery to rats

  • Precise delivery using a variety of controllers
  • Adaptable and easily customizable
  • Prevents contamination by having odors far downstreamr

Easy cleaning, maintenance, and replacement of parts

  • Multiple parts are designed to be used and replaced" Odor valves are teflon and easy to clean
  • Laminar flow allows for precise odor delivery without the confounds of somatosensation

Robust and manufacturable

  • Custom parts are designed to be machined out of hard plastic or aluminum by a typical machine shop. A less durable version could be 3D printed
  • Zero exposed wires, all connections are integrated into the PCB or made with solder joints.
    • Removes possibility of connections coming loose or wires becoming damaged over time.
  • System can be scaled up and down to manage odor needs.
    • Single sided PCB with surface mount components is ideal for a PCB assembly service or manual assembly with a toaster oven/hot plate/heat gun.

Open source

  • All files are available on GitHub and can be modified to meet custom needs
